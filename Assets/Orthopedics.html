Faq's

What services does Medicare offer?

At Medicare, we offer a wide range of medical services, including cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, cancer treatment, ophthalmology, and respiratory care. Our team is dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate care for all your health needs.

How can I book an appointment?

Booking an appointment is easy. You can schedule your visit through our online appointment system on our website, or you can call our reception directly. We strive to make the process as convenient as possible for you.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, your insurance card, and any relevant medical records or test results. If you are a new patient, it may also be helpful to bring a list of any medications you are currently taking.

Does Medicare accept my insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans. If you have any questions about your specific coverage, please contact our billing department, and we will be happy to assist you.

How do I get my test results?

Test results are typically available within a few days. You can access your results through our secure patient portal, or you will receive a call from our office once your results are ready.

What are the office hours?

Our office is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays. For emergencies, please visit the nearest hospital or call 911.
